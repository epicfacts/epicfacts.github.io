LGBT

Epic Facts, as opposed to Hate Facts.

Transgender + Nonbinary

  • Medical transition decreases dysphoria, suicide attempts, depression (https://whatweknow.inequality.cornell.edu/topics/lgbt-equality/what-does-the-scholarly-research-say-about-the-well-being-of-transgender-people/)
  • Trans men, trans women and non-binary people all have similar representation in the US Population (https://transequality.org/sites/default/files/docs/usts/USTS-Full-Report-Dec17.pdf)

Puberty Blockers

  • Puberty blockers are effective at reducing dysphoria, anxiety, and depression (https://academic.oup.com/jcem/article/102/11/3869/4157558)
  • Puberty blockers do not harm bone density (https://www.eurekalert.org/pub_releases/2013-06/tes-mii061513.php)

Mental health

  • 41% is the attempt rate, not the successful suicide rate (https://www.transequality.org/sites/default/files/docs/resources/NTDS_Report.pdf)
  • Victimization increases the attempt rate (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5905855/)
  • Family support and acceptance decreases suicide attempts and drug usage (https://whatweknow.inequality.cornell.edu/topics/lgbt-equality/what-does-the-scholarly-research-say-about-the-acceptancerejection-of-lgbt-youth-2/)
